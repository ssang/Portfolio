about me

I am a 22 year old Korean boy born in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Now I'm living in Langley, BC. I am currently studying at Simon Fraser University as a Computer Science major. My main point of interest is web-development and more specifically backend management. I recently finished the Techinical Web Designer program at BCIT and have fully put my foot into the web development world.

When I'm not staring intensly at page full of code and markup, I may still be staring at the computer screen "clearing my mind" with a game or browsing the internet. When I'm actually away from my keyboard, I'm usually found taking walks or hanging out with friends playing a game of basketball or volleybal. I'm a big dog lover, and very much enjoy going for long drives. I am a follower of Christ and have been raised in the church for the entirety of my life. I currently attend Vancouver Hebron Presbyterian Church.

Services

There's nothing more enjoyable for me in this line of work than pushing myself to learn something new. This is an ever advancing field, and the only way to stay competitive is to be advancing my skillsets as well. Taking that motivation, I try hard to keep my skills up to date with this fast-paced industry.

One thing is for certain, I am not a designer in that I am not able to give you a fresh new look for your website. But I do have many opinions when it comes to design and User Experience. In terms of skillsets that I offer, PHP and MySQL integration is my main focus. HTML, CSS and Javascript (along with jQuery) for front-end work is also something that I am proficient in. Lastly, Wordpress is something that been recently added to my repitoire, and I can now take your design and turn that into a working Wordpress Template.
